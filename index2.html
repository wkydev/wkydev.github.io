More About WKYDev

Our hope is that we can foster a community of developers across a wide range of disciplines. Western Kentucky has a large number of smart and experienced developers, and we enjoy getting together in the same room every once in a while to help each other and newcomers to the industry. We welcome people of all skill levels and interest, so please come even if you are new to the field.
