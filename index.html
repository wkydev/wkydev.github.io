Western Kentucky Developers User Group

The Western Kentucky Developers User Group is a group for people in the Western Kentucky area to get together and share and discuss ideas around software development. We meet the second Monday of every month between September and December, and between February and May. Please view the about page for more information.

November 12th Meeting

We'll be having our monthly meeting next week on November 12th at 6pm. It will be in room 552 of the Business Building on Murray State's campus. Duane Dycus will be speaking on "Life As A Security Analyst." Duane is a senior security analyst at Murray State, and I'm sure he'll have lots of interesting things to share.

We'll provide pizza and drinks as usual. Looking forward to seeing you there.
